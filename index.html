To go up one spot on the leaderboard, you have to verse the person above or the person on the same rank as you. You must play 9 rounds with them:
  • 3 Rivals
  • 3 MVSD
  • 3 Bedwars
In Rivals, if you are using autofire, you have to turn aimassist completely off so it is fair for PC players. You also have to use the following weapons unless both agree not to:
  • Assault Rifle
  • Handgun
  • Fists
  • Grenade
In MVSD, you have to use no abilities unless you both agree not to.
In Bedwars, you have to use no kit unless you both agree not to.
